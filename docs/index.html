Livery Lens is the web port of the livery-lens Python CLI — a spike: expect a useful starting point, not a finished livery. Sponsor logos and vectorization are out of scope. The in-browser model is the small u2netp; masks are slightly rougher than the CLI's full-size U2-Net. Everything runs locally in your browser. Source · third-party licenses
